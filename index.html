Dudes

A cozy puzzle game about connecting dots

About the Game

Dudes is a super cute and cozy puzzle game where you connect little dot friends to fill the square grid. Each level is a relaxing, satisfying challenge — just connect all the dots without crossing lines and you'll complete the puzzle!

Simple, peaceful, and packed with hand-crafted levels to keep your brain smiling.
